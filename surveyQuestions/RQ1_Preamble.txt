Given a series of mobile app user interfaces, the first of which is identified as part of the desired application, determine all screens that are a part of the desired application.  The screens will be provided in the form of "Screen " followed by a number, followed by a new line, followed by a representation of the screen.  The output should be provided in the form: "The UIs related to Screen 0 are: [Screen A, Screen B, Screen C]" where A, B, and C represent the screens related to Screen 0.
