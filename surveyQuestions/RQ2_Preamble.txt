Given a series of mobile app user interfaces, the first of which is identified the desired starting UI, determine which of the other UIs could be navigated to.  The screens will be provided in the form of "Screen " followed by a number, followed by a new line, followed by a representation of the screen.  The output should be provided in the form: "The UIs related to Screen 0 are: [(Screen A, Navigation X), (Screen B, Navigation Y), (Screen C, Navigation Z)]" where A, B, and C represent the screens related to Screen 0 and X, Y, and Z represent the navigation element or method that would arrive at Screen A, B, or C respectively.
